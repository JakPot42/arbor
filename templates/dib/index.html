Enter a company name to retrieve its SEC 10-K financial data, run a Monte Carlo distress simulation, and screen for CFIUS-relevant ownership changes.

Policy Context

The DoD Industrial Base Policy office tracks DIB financial health and foreign ownership in its Annual Industrial Capabilities Report to Congress. This tool automates that analysis using public SEC filings.
